## ROOT CAUSE OF THEIR PROBLEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

**The Revelation (This is the key differentiator):**

> "Rynker og fine linjer er ikke problemet. De er symptomer."
> — Copy framework

**The Root Cause:** A weakened skin barrier.

**What is the skin barrier?**
The thin, invisible layer that holds your skin together. When functioning optimally, it keeps moisture IN and irritants OUT. Your skin looks plump, feels elastic, has a natural glow.

**What damages the skin barrier?**
- Age (natural degradation)
- Pollution
- Stress
- Hormonal changes (especially menopause)
- Wrong skincare products
- Sun damage

**What happens when the barrier weakens?**
→ Moisture evaporates constantly — no matter how much cream you apply
→ Skin becomes vulnerable — redness, irritation, sensitivity
→ Fine lines deepen — because skin can't maintain its structure
→ Products stop working — they treat symptoms, not the cause

**The Analogy:**

> "At smøre fugtcreme på en svækket hudbarriere er som at hælde vand i en spand med hul i bunden."
