## Background

The current formula SBW006-05 delivers good results on wrinkles, tolerance, and consumer satisfaction. We want to preserve the fundamentals of the formula.

There are **three technical issues** we need to address:

### 1. TEWL data is not satisfactory
Tewameter data from Zurko (28-day study, n=21, formula SBW006-05) showed a statistically significant TEWL increase of +11% after 4 hours (p=0.04), and no significant TEWL reduction at D14 or D28. This means we cannot communicate clinically documented strengthening of the skin barrier — the core promise of the brand name "Barrier Defense".

### 2. Fragrance is a real consumer problem
We receive recurring complaints from customers about the fragrance. Descriptions include "smoky", "overpowering", "bad smell". This is one of the most frequent negative feedback points and impacts both repurchase rates and word-of-mouth.

We also suspect that the fragrance changes over time after the product has been stored — including when unopened. We do not have 100% proof of this, but there are several indications pointing in this direction.

### 3. Wrinkle reduction needs to improve
The current formula achieved 27% reduction at D28 (PRIMOS-CR). We want to improve this result in BDS 2.0.

As a starting point for discussion, we are considering the following strategy (S&J determines the technical solution):

- **Increase peptide concentrations to clinically documented levels.** Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 (Argireline) and Palmitoyl Tripeptide-38 (Matrixyl Synthe'6) appear at the end of the current INCI list, indicating low concentrations. Clinical literature shows significant wrinkle reduction with Matrixyl Synthe'6 at 2% and Argireline at 5-10%.
- **Add Hydroxypinacolone Retinoate (HPR / Granactive Retinoid).** HPR binds directly to retinoid receptors without enzymatic conversion and has clinically documented complementary effects with retinol via dual pathway activation.
- **Maintain encapsulated retinol (ROVISOME) at current concentration.** This is the core that delivered the 27% result.
- **Maintain bakuchiol** (synergistic effect with retinol).

## Requirements for BDS 2.0

### Must achieve
1. **Statistically significant TEWL reduction at D28** (p<0.05), and no significant TEWL increase at D0+4h
2. **Improved fragrance profile** — neutral or pleasant scent without the "smoky" / "rotten" undertones customers report
3. **Higher wrinkle reduction than 27% at D28** on PRIMOS-CR (p<0.05)

### Must maintain
- M.I.I. ≤ 0.200 (Non-Irritating) on patch test
- Texture (lightweight, easily absorbed serum)
- Packaging (same bottle)
- Premium ingredient quality

## Testing after reformulation

Same protocol as BDS 1.0:

1. **Stability test 12 weeks** (S&J internal, same S&J MASTER TYPE C protocol as SBW006-05) — accelerated stability at 4°C, RT, 40°C, 50°C, solar box, freeze-thaw cycles
2. **PET / Challenge Test 28 days** (ISO 11930:2019, same method as BDS 1.0)
3. **Patch test 96 hours occlusive** (n≥30, same method as Zurko VV_CC-PT/30D3T1)
4. **28-day efficacy study at Zurko Research** (same protocol as VV_ET-16/20DXT3) with:
   - Tewameter® TM 300 (TEWL — critical endpoint)
   - PRIMOS-CR® (wrinkles — critical endpoint, target: better than 27%)
   - Subjective evaluation (including fragrance assessment)
   - Dermatological tolerance
