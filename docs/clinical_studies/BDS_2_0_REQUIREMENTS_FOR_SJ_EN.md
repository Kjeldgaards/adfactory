# BDS 2.0 — Brief for S&J

**To:** Khun Natcha, S&J International Enterprises
**From:** Thomas Kjeldgaard, KJELDGAARD ApS
**Product:** Barrier Defense® Serum 2.0 (reformulation of SBW006-05)

---

Due to the high popularity of BDS 1.0, we are planning to launch a BDS 2.0 with a few optimisations.

---

## 1. Scent

We receive too many complaints about the scent, mostly described as smoky, sewer-like, or simply disgusting.

In addition, it feels like the scent changes over time, which might be worth keeping in consideration.

We would like R&D to look into the formula and suggest the best way to change the scent — preferably without perfume or oils if possible.

If R&D has clarification questions, we are happy to answer anything that could benefit development.

---

## 2. Optimise the Tewameter data

Tewameter data from Zurko (28-day study, n=21, formula SBW006-05) showed a statistically significant TEWL increase of +11% after 4 hours (p=0.04), and no significant TEWL reduction at D14 or D28.

Based on our research, this means we cannot communicate clinically documented strengthening of the skin barrier — the core promise of the brand name "Barrier Defense". *Please correct us if this interpretation is wrong.*

---

## 3. Improve wrinkle reduction

The current formula achieved 27% reduction at D28 (PRIMOS-CR). We want to improve this result in BDS 2.0.

As a starting point for discussion, we are considering the following strategy (these are only suggestions — as always, we trust R&D's final decision):

- **Increase peptide concentrations to clinically documented levels.** Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 (Argireline) and Palmitoyl Tripeptide-38 (Matrixyl Synthe'6) appear at the end of the current INCI list, indicating low concentrations. Clinical literature shows significant wrinkle reduction with Matrixyl Synthe'6 at 2% and Argireline at 5-10%.
- **Add Hydroxypinacolone Retinoate (HPR / Granactive Retinoid).** HPR binds directly to retinoid receptors without enzymatic conversion and has clinically documented complementary effects with retinol via dual pathway activation.
- **Maintain encapsulated retinol (ROVISOME)** at current concentration — this is the core that delivered the 27% result.
- **Maintain bakuchiol** (synergistic effect with retinol).

---

## Requirements for BDS 2.0 — must achieve

1. Statistically significant TEWL reduction at D28 (p<0.05), and no significant TEWL increase at D0+4h
2. Improved fragrance profile — neutral or pleasant scent without the "smoky" / "rotten" undertones customers report
3. Higher wrinkle reduction than 27% at D28 on PRIMOS-CR (p<0.05)
